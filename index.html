Hollow Knight: Silksong Delayed Again

Team Cherry cites a last-minute bug discovery as the reason for the unexpected setback.

By Alex Turner | September 3, 2025

Fans eagerly awaiting the release of Hollow Knight: Silksong will have to wait a little longer. Developer Team Cherry announced today that the game’s launch has been delayed due to the discovery of a critical last-minute bug.

“We want Silksong to be as polished and enjoyable as possible,” Team Cherry wrote in a statement. “Unfortunately, a complex issue surfaced during final testing that affects gameplay stability. We need additional time to address this before release.”

This marks yet another delay in the long-anticipated follow-up to Hollow Knight. While Team Cherry has not provided a new release date, they assured fans that development is progressing steadily and that the delay is necessary to uphold the game’s quality standards.

Despite the setback, excitement remains high for Silksong, which promises an expansive new world, hundreds of enemies, and Hornet’s fast-paced acrobatics. For now, fans will have to keep waiting for the highly anticipated adventure.
